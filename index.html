 About our project :

Learning managements systems such as Canvas and Moodle use a linear class data layout. By this, we mean that class schedules and topics are organized by weeks, and class modules, links, and assignments are organized into lists. Furthermore, most classes are taught in a boring way. Educators lecture and hopefully students listen. We have a better way. Explore Learning Universe.
