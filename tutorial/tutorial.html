Creating an Account

From the Learning Universe homepage click on "Register". You will prompted for your account information. Make sure to correctly choose your role. Fill out the form and complete registration.

Customizing your Profile

Click on the account icon on the navbar and click on profile from the dropdown to navigate to your profile page. Alternatively, from the account homepage click on your default profile picture (center node). Customize your profile by modifying the fields. You can even upload your photo. Click "Submit" when done making changes.

Creating a Class

Creating a class is allowed only to professors. From the account homepage click on the plus node. You will be promted for your class information. Use the enrollment key to restrict access. One the class has been created it will show up on your account homepage.

Adding a Class

From your account homepage click on the plus node. You will be asked to select your class from a dropdown and enter an enrollment key if necessary.

Building a Class

From your account home click on the class you wish to build. If you have not created a class you can learn how to do that here. Remeber to use the tooltips by hovering over items on the toolbar, or the help messages seen by clicking on the help icon on the navbar. The first view that you will be customizing is the class view. This is a place for all high level concepts. Drag nodes from the toolbar onto the map and name them to represent high level concepts.

Click the save button when done to save the map configuration. One the map has been saved fill out the information for each node by clicking on the page icon. Here you can set due dates, available dates, descriptions, and more.

One the high level map has been constructed click on a node page and click "navigate to concept" to navigate to the low level concept page. Repeat the previous steps described keeping in mind that the low level concept page is for low level concepts, assignments, and quizzes. Again, on this new page refer to the tooltips and help messages.

Viewing a Class

Student
From your account homepage click on the class you wish to view. If you have not added the class you can find instructions on how to do that here. You will be redirected to the class homepage. Here you can find information on all high level concepts. Click on the page icon to see more information on each node. Navigate to the concept view by clicking on a node. Each high level node has a set of low level nodes connected to it. These low level nodes can be concepts, quizzes, or assignments. Complete a high level node by completing every sub node. The percent on the class homepage will tell you what percentage of sub nodes have been completed.

Click on help icon on the navbar to see more information on both the class homepage and the low level concept page.

Professor
From the class builder click the class view link on the navbar. You will be redirected to the professor class view. From this page you can click on page icons to view class statistics. Click on a node to navigate to the concept view. Again, click on page icons to view node statistics.

Grades

Student
From the class homepage click on the "Grades" link on the navbar. You will be redirected to your grades page. Click on an assignment or quiz bar to expand it and view the grading charts.

Professor
From the professor concept view click on an assignment node to open up the grade page. Here you can edit the grades for all students in the class. Alternatively, you can simply view grade charts by clicking on the various page icons to open up the node information.

Messaging

Student
From the account homepage click on the message icon (top right). Select the class from the drop down and fill in a message to send to the professor.

Professor
From the account homepage click on the message icon (top right). Slect a class from the drop down and fill in a message to send to your class.

Both
Each node has a discussion section. Click on the page icon from one of the map views to particpate in this discussion.

Notifications

From your account homepage click on the notification icon (top right) to see a list of recent notifications or messages.
