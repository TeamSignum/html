About Learning Universe

Learning Universe changes the structure of learning management sites. We use a "learning map" to show information in a connected and meaningful way. Topics are advantageously broken down into small chunks that can be completed and checked-off. We also help students visualize where their fellow classmates are in the course using participants nodes. We provide professors with tools to visualize the progress of students and tools to easily build a learning map. Of course, both professors and students can view grades, construct and takes quizzes, and discuss topics with each other in discussion forums. Thank you for your interest in Learning Universe.
